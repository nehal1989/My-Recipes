For 4 people
1 Cauliflower
2-3 potatoes (3 if small potatoes)
1 aubergine
1 cup peas
1 carrot
2 small or 1 large onions chopped
0.5 tinned chopped tomatoes

Spices
1 tsp cumin + coriander power
0.5 tsp garam masala
salt
sugar to taste
cinnamon stick
0.5 tsp red chilli powder

1) Boil cauliflower, potato, aubgergine, carrot, peas
2) Fry cinnamon, then add onion, add spices and red chilli powder, salt
3) Add chopped tomatoes
4) Cook until oil separates
5) Then add boiled vegetables and mash
6) Simmer gently, add 1 spoon butter


Serve with lemon and fresh coriander
