Name: Souvlaki Spice Mix

Source of recipe:ATCO Blue Flame Kitchen

Use: Use spice mix for marinading chicken, lamb, fish. Can mix with yoghurt to form a nice marinading mixture.

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons dried oregano
2 tablespoons paprika
2 teaspoons thyme
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teapsoon garlic powder (can use fresh garlic crushed if don't have)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
