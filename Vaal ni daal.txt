Vaal ni daal

250g Vaal daal
1 tsp ajwan 
1 tablespoon oil
5 garlic cloves chopped
1 green chilli
0.25 teaspoon tumeric
0.5 teaspoon salt
1.5 cup water (or 1.5x times volume of daal)

1) Soak vaal daal in water for minimum 30 minutes.
2) Fry ajwan in oil with garlic
3) Add tumeric, chilli, vaal in daal (drained and rinsed) and salt
4) Cook in pressure cooker on medium pressure for 10 minutes. Check if done. Cook for 5 min longer if needed and add water.
